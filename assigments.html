Assignment III: Me of the Screen

A digital recreation of the interaction between myself and podcasts on Spotify. I often listen to podcasts as I try to fall asleep. The interaction helps me clear my mind of anxious thoughts and fall asleep faster. Over a period of a week, I documented the qualitative and quantitative qualities of my interactions with podcasts to compile the data I've gathered into an infographic.
